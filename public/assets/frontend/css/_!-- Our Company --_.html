Our Company

Welcome to Liquor Junction – Your Ultimate Destination for Fine Spirits!

Established in 2021 by the visionary entrepreneur Sunny Punjabi, Liquor Junction has rapidly become West Africa's premier retail chain for all things spirits. With branches strategically scattered across the region, our commitment to providing an unparalleled selection of beverages has made us a favorite among discerning customers.
